Research updates and publication

Fire risk management:

My PhD dissertation is on data-driven modeling for fire risk management. Specifically, I am working on spatio-temporal prediction and resource allocation models to mitigate losses/risk due to structural fires. The following is a paper on some work done so far:

  • Adam Behrendt, Vineet M. Payyappalli, Jun Zhuang. Modeling the Cost Effectiveness of Fire Protection Resource Allocation in the United States: Models and a 1980-2014 Case Study. Working paper (under revision), University at Buffalo.

Publications:

  • Peiqiu Guan, Jing Zhang, Vineet M. Payyappalli, and Jun Zhuang. 2018. Modeling and Validating Public-Private Partnerships in Disaster Management – A Sequential Game Considering Risk Preferences. Decision Analysis. Vol: 15. No: 2. Pages: 55-131.
  • Vineet M. Payyappalli, Adam Behrendt, and Kathryn Lukasiewicz. 2017. Total Cost of Fire in the United States. Technical Report, National Fire Protection Association.
  • Vineet M. Payyappalli, Jun Zhuang, and Victor Richmond R. Jose. 2017. Deterrence and Risk Preferences in Sequential Attacker-Defender Games with Continuous Efforts. Risk Analysis. Vol: 37. No: 11. Pages: 2229-2245.
