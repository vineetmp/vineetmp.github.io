Projects:

1) National Institute of Technology Calicut Alumni Mentoring Program (NITCAMP): From April 2017, I have been working with other alumni of my alma mater National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC), India, to develop a mentoring program for current students at NITC. The program provides a platform for NITC students to interact with alumni who could provide guidance regarding higher studies, job search, career, and general professional development. We successfully completed one year, during which, 90+ students interacted with 40+ alumni. This year, I developed an algorithm for matching students and alumni based on their individual preferences. This automated matching was necessary as the number of mentors available (40+) was significantly less than the number of students interested (500+)! The algorithm is available in my GitHub page here.
